Kernel notes.

NEON kernels on the A76 are vmlaq-bound for Q4_K prefill; register spills,
decode-style tiling and batched scales all failed to reach parity. Parity
needs fp32 BLAS.

Activation rotation: the packed 4-bit KV cache uses an activation rotation
so keys quantize with lower error at depth. Split KL by context depth in
the quality bench to see the effect.
